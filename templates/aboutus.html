About Us

A Library Management System (LMS) automates library tasks like book tracking, member management, borrowing, and returns, streamlining operations for staff and providing easy access to resources for users.

A Library Management System (LMS) is a comprehensive software application developed to efficiently manage various library operations, such as tracking books, managing member records, and facilitating borrowing and returning processes. It offers a digital solution that replaces traditional manual methods, allowing library staff to maintain an organized database of books, students, and staff activities.
